Doha Circuit

Track Length: 5.380 km

First Grand Prix: 2021

Notable Features: Mix of technical corners and fast straights. Known for its unique design and high-speed challenge.

The Doha Circuit is a highly technical track that balances sharp corners with high-speed straights, making it a unique challenge for the drivers. The layout is designed for maximum racing excitement, testing drivers' cornering skills and speed management.

Notable Moments: The 2021 Qatar Grand Prix was a thrilling race, showcasing tire management and skillful overtaking strategies. Max Verstappen's victory in this race further solidified his grip on the 2021 title.

Red Sea Circuit

Track Length: 6.100 km

First Grand Prix: 2022

Notable Features: A combination of technical corners and long straights along the Red Sea coast.

Located along the scenic Red Sea coastline, this circuit presents a mix of challenging corners and long straights, providing an exciting and dynamic racing experience for both drivers and spectators.

Notable Moments: The inaugural 2022 race saw dramatic performances from both seasoned drivers and new talents, making it a memorable start for the circuit.
